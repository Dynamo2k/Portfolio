## Competition Strategies

1. **Team coordination** - Divide challenges by expertise
2. **Time management** - Prioritize high-point challenges
3. **Tool preparation** - Have your toolkit ready before the competition
4. **Stay calm** - Pressure can lead to mistakes
5. **Learn from every competition** - Win or lose, there's always something to learn
